Far Cry 3 and Assassin's Creed board games are heavily discounted on Prime Big Deal Days.

Amazon is offering fantastic savings on strategy board games based on two of Ubisoft's most popular properties, Far Cry and Assassin's Creed. Far Cry: Escape from Rook Islands was only launched in August, but Amazon's Prime Big Deal Days event allows members to purchase it for a much lower price than the game's $65 list price. You can get Far Cry: Escape from Rook Islands for just $25. You could use the $40 you save on the Far Cry board game to get Assassin's Creed: Brotherhood of Venice, which is now on sale for $40.

These are all-time low pricing for both games, but you must be a Prime member to receive the savings, and Amazon has limited quantities of both titles--we wouldn't be surprised if they ran out before Big Deal Days finishes on October 9. Other video game-inspired board games, such as Civilization: A New Dawn, are qualified for Amazon's toy and game deal, which offers an additional $10 off $40 orders and $20 off $75 or more.
